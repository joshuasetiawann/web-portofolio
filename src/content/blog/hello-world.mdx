This is where I think out loud — about engineering, craft, and the strange, fun
overlap between the two.

## What lives here

Expect notes on building for the web with intent: performance budgets that hold,
design systems that scale, motion that respects the people using it, and the
occasional deep dive into a problem that refused to be simple.

Some posts will be practical and code-heavy. Others will be closer to a sketch —
an idea worth writing down before it evaporates.

## How to read it

There is no schedule and no manifesto. If something here saves you an afternoon,
sparks an idea, or just makes the work feel a little more human, that is enough.

Thanks for stopping by.
